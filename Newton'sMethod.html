Newton's Method

An Interactive Applet powered by Sage and MathJax.

(By Kelsey Norman with HTML code from Prof. Gregory V. Bard)

\textrm{An interactive demo to show the iterations of Newton's method to find the roots of a function, given by the formula} \\ x_1 = x_0 - \frac{f(x_0)}{f'(x_0)}.
